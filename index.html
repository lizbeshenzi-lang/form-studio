/ 001 — The studio

Movement as practice, not punishment

Form was opened in 2018 by two movement specialists who wanted to create a studio that treated the body as something to be understood, not exhausted. We were tired of gyms where the goal was maximum output and minimum thought.

Every session at Form begins with an assessment of how you are moving — not how much weight you can lift. We work with postural imbalances, chronic tension, recovery from injury, and the kind of low-grade stiffness that accumulates when you sit at a desk for ten years.
